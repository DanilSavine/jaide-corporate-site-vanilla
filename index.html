Our Project

AI Clinical Assistant

jaide develops an intelligent clinical assistant that accompanies doctors in their daily practice. The solution enables healthcare providers to automate note-taking and generation of medical reports using artificial intelligence.

Objective: enable healthcare providers to save time to focus on what matters most: patients.

AI Prognostic Models

jaide also develops advanced artificial intelligence models to predict cancer evolution and improve therapeutic decision-making. These models can eventually be integrated into the AI clinical assistant for oncology.

Prostate Cancer

Interpretable predictive model for 10-year disease evolution in patients with prostate cancer.

Accuracy (auROC): 80%

Colorectal Cancer

Interpretable predictive model for 10-year disease evolution in patients with colorectal cancer.

Accuracy (auROC): 84%

Lung Cancer

Multimodal predictive model for early assessment of non-response to tyrosine kinase inhibitors in patients with EGFR+ lung cancer.

In development

Objective: Develop reliable predictive tools to improve personalized care for cancer patients and optimize therapeutic strategies.
