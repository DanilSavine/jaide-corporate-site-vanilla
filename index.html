Artificial Intelligence that cares

Book a demo

jaide seamlessly integrates into the patient-physician journey to enhance decision-making ‍

Between medical visits

Patients quickly fill tailor-made intake forms, seamlessly upload test results and interact with AI to provide comprehensive and structured information to doctors.

During medical visits

Doctors get AI Medical Report, predictive modeling on disease evolution and clinical recommendations based on latest institutional standards to ease documentation and provide world-class care to patients.

Benefits

Patients

  • Access best possible care
  • Facilitate communication with physicians
  • Improve engagement

Doctors

  • Support clinical decision
  • Save time to focus on what matters
  • Make documentation easy

Institutions

  • Improve health outcomes for patients
  • Reduce risks of medical misevaluation
  • Manage compliant & interoperable data
